Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Pol Estrella ("we", "our", or "us") built the Basketball Rotation Timer app (the "App") as a free application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter (player names, game settings, payment status) is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

Camera Permission

The App may request access to your device's camera for the purpose of importing player names using text recognition (OCR). Images captured are processed locally on your device and are not stored, uploaded, or shared.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when a rotation ends. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Storage

All game data, player information, and settings are stored locally on your device using a local database. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us. Uninstalling the App will delete all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
